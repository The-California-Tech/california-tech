The [directive](https://www.ed.gov/media/document/dear-colleague-letter-sffa-v-harvard-109506.pdf), attributed to Acting Assistant Secretary for Civil Rights Craig Trainor, prescribes that all universities receiving federal funding have until February 28th to stop providing race-conscious social justice or equity programming before the Education Department begins ‚Äúassess[ing] compliance‚Ä¶ based on the understanding embodied in this letter.‚Äù The order suggests that ‚Äúinstitutions that fail to comply‚Ä¶ face potential loss of federal funding.‚Äù

Caltech is a major destination for federal research spending, with total grants exceeding $221.7 million in 2024.
